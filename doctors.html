Our Doctors

Qualified Healthcare Professionals

Dr. Aroop Mukherjee

MBBS, MS – Orthopedics

Dr Mukherjee is an eminent orthopaedist based in New Delhi. He specializes in lower extremity wound care, bone reconstruction, ACL reconstruction, limb lengthening, etc.He has an overall experience of 34 years and practices at Max Multi Speciality Centre, Panchsheel Park, Delhi.


Dr. Anandibai Joshi

first female Indian physician

Dr. Anandibai Joshi was the first female Indian physician. She was also the first Indian woman to have gained a degree in western medicine. She qualified as a doctor by gaining a degree from Women’s Medical College. This college is now known as Drexel University in the United States of America.


Dr. Ajaya Nand Jha

MBBS, MS, FRCS – Neurosurgery

Dr. Jha is an eminent neurosurgeon and has specialized in the use of intraoperative MRI and the use of ALA dyes in cerebrovascular and tumor surgeries.He has also contributed greatly to the field of stroke, epilepsy, and movement disorders. He has 42 years of experience including 33 years as a neuro specialist. He practices at Medanta Medicity.


Dr. Padmavati Iyer

Cardiology Specialist

Often touted as the god of cardiology in India, Dr Padmavati Sivaramakrishna Iyer turned 101 this year. She is as active now as she was when she started training patients in India 60 plus years ago. Her profound knowledge and unbridled enthusiasm helped her create the whole concept of heart treatment in India from scratch.
